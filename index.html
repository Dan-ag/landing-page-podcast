Sean Paul - Francis Henriques (born 9 January 1973)

Known professionally as Sean Paul, is a Jamaican dancehall rapper, singer and record producer..

Sean Paul was born in Kingston, Jamaica to parents Garth and Frances. His mother is a well-known painter. His paternal grandfather is from a Jewish family that had emigrated from Portugal..
